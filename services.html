Our Services

The Albanian Islamic Center provides a comprehensive range of religious and community services to support our members through all stages of life. Our experienced staff and volunteers are here to assist you with compassion and dedication.

Marriage Services (Nikkah)

We provide complete Islamic marriage services including pre-marital counseling, Nikkah ceremonies, and marriage documentation. Our qualified imams conduct ceremonies in accordance with Islamic principles and Michigan state requirements.

What's Included:

  • Pre-marital counseling sessions
  • Nikkah ceremony with qualified imam
  • Marriage certificate preparation
  • Venue arrangement (mosque or off-site)
  • Islamic guidance and consultation

Requirements:

  • Valid Michigan marriage license
  • Two adult male witnesses or one male and two female witnesses
  • Advance appointment scheduling
  • Pre-marital counseling session (recommended)
Duration: 30-60 minutes
Fee: Donation-based

Funeral Services

Our community provides comprehensive funeral services including Janazah prayers, burial arrangements, and family support during difficult times. We guide families through Islamic funeral rites with compassion and care.

What's Included:

  • Ghusl (ritual washing) coordination
  • Kafan (shrouding) preparation
  • Janazah prayer (Salat al-Janazah)
  • Burial coordination at Muslim cemetery
  • Family counseling and support
  • Community meal coordination

Available 24/7:

  • Emergency contact for immediate needs
  • Coordination with funeral homes
  • Islamic guidance during mourning period
  • Community support for grieving families
Availability: 24/7 Emergency
Fee: No charge
