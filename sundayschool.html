Programs & Activities

Learn about our ongoing events, educational programs, and community initiatives.

Sunday School Programs

Our Sunday School is dedicated to providing a comprehensive Islamic education for children and youth. We offer classes that cover Quranic studies, Islamic teachings, and cultural heritage, fostering a strong foundation in faith and community values. Classes are held every Sunday from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM.

Quranic Studies & Tajweed

Our Quranic studies program is designed to introduce children to the holy Quran and its teachings. Students learn proper recitation with Tajweed rules, memorization techniques, and understanding of Quranic Arabic. Classes are taught by qualified instructors who provide individual attention to each student.

Age Groups: 6-18 years
Time: Sundays 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Instructor: Sheikh Shuajb Gerguri

Fiqh & Aqeedah Classes

Fiqh classes provide in-depth knowledge of Islamic jurisprudence, helping students understand the practical applications of Islamic law in daily life. Aqeedah classes focus on Islamic beliefs and theology, strengthening students' understanding of fundamental Islamic concepts and faith.

Age Groups: 10-18 years
Time: Sundays 12:30 PM - 2:00 PM
Instructor: Sheikh Shuajb Gerguri

Albanian Language & Heritage

The Albanian language class is a valuable opportunity for children to learn the Albanian language, fostering a deep understanding of Albanian culture and heritage. Students learn reading, writing, speaking, and cultural traditions that connect them to their roots.

Age Groups: 6-16 years
Time: Sundays 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM
Instructor: Community Volunteers

Albanian Cultural Activities

Our cultural program introduces children to traditional Albanian folk dances, music, and customs. Students participate in performances during special events and holidays, learning about their heritage through engaging activities and cultural celebrations.

Age Groups: 5-18 years
Time: Sundays 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM
Activities: Folk dance, music, traditional crafts

Recreational Facilities

Basketball & Volleyball Court

Our basketball and volleyball court is a vibrant space where children and youth can play, exercise, and learn together. The facility promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and healthy competition.
