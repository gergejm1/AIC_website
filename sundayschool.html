Programs & Activities

Learn about our ongoing events, educational programs, and community initiatives.

Sunday School

Our Sunday School is dedicated to providing a comprehensive Islamic education for children and youth. We offer classes that cover Quranic studies, Islamic teachings, and cultural heritage, fostering a strong foundation in faith and community values.

Quranic Studies

Our Quranic studies program is designed to introduce children to the holy Quran and its teachings.

Fiqh and Aqeedah Classes

Fiqh classes provide in-depth knowledge of Islamic jurisprudence, helping students understand the practical applications of Islamic law in daily life.

Albanian Language Class

The Albanian language class is a valuable opportunity for children to learn the Albanian language, fostering a deep understanding of the Albanian culture and heritage.

Basketball/Volleyball Court

Our basketball court is a vibrant space where children can play and learn together.

Welcome to the Albanian Islamic Center, a place of worship, learning, and community rooted in rich Balkan heritage and Islamic tradition. We strive to foster a welcoming and inclusive space for every member of the Muslim community.
