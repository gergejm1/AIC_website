Frequently Asked Questions

When does my membership begin?

Your membership begins immediately upon payment confirmation and remains active for 12 months from the start date.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options. You can pay quarterly ($50 per quarter) or monthly ($20 per month) with prior arrangement.

What if I move during my membership?

Your membership remains valid even if you relocate. We can update your address and send materials to your new location.

Are there volunteer opportunities?

Absolutely! Members are encouraged to participate in community service projects, event planning, and center maintenance activities.
